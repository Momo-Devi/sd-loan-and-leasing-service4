Novated Lease: A Smart and Tax-Efficient Way to Own a Vehicle

What is a Novated Lease?

A Novated Lease is a salary packaging arrangement where an employer agrees to make lease payments on behalf of an employee from their pre-tax salary. This provides significant tax savings while offering a cost-effective way to own a car.

Key Benefits of a Novated Lease

  • Tax Savings: Lease payments are deducted from pre-tax income, reducing taxable salary.
  • Flexible Lease Terms: Choose a lease period that suits your needs.
  • Lower Running Costs: Includes fuel, maintenance, registration, and insurance in one package.
  • Vehicle Choice: Select any car that fits within your budget and preferences.
  • Ownership Options: At the end of the lease, choose to buy, upgrade, or return the vehicle.

How Does a Novated Lease Work?

The process is simple and advantageous for employees:

  1. Select a vehicle and negotiate the purchase price.
  2. Sign a Novation Agreement between you, your employer, and the leasing provider.
  3. Your employer deducts lease payments from your pre-tax salary.
  4. Enjoy a fully maintained vehicle with included costs.
  5. At the end of the lease, decide whether to upgrade, refinance, or purchase the car outright.

Who Should Consider a Novated Lease?

A Novated Lease is ideal for employees looking to maximize their salary benefits while enjoying a new car without the hassle of upfront payments and ongoing expenses.
